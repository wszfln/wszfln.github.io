Project Description

My project is an Android-based mobile application designed to simplify and enhance the waste sorting and recycling process. The application, called GreenSort, provides users with an interactive platform with three functional modules available. In the waste classification knowledge introduction module, users can obtain knowledge about waste management. In the garbage classification query module and garbage collection point positioning module, the program utilizes advanced image recognition and positioning technology to enable users to immediately identify waste categories and find nearby recycling centers, thus contributing to a more sustainable environment. At the same time, the app provides real-time data and a comprehensive database of waste categories, improving recycling accuracy and efficiency. Importantly, GreenSort is not only for residents but also ideal for tourists. People on vacation can use the app to responsibly dispose of their waste before leaving a city or vacation spot, significantly reducing their carbon footprint and promoting eco-friendly tourism practices. In summary，GreenSort's features are accessible to everyone in Ireland who needs to sort and recycle waste, aiming to tackle poor waste management and encourage environmentally friendly practices.
